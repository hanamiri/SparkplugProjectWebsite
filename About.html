Ajogun Naomi is a recent graduate of Igbinedion University, Okada where she studied Medical Laboratory Science. she is the second of three children and the only girl child of her parents. she is a feminist and also a passion driven woman. She was born in Benin city, Edo state on the 16th of June, 1995. She believes tech is the future and Nigeria needs to wake up or get left behind and this has been the inspiration behind her delving into the tech scene. She hopes to run for government office one day to make Nigeria a better place for all.
